# Artifacts

Document types that can appear in the novel. Artifacts serve as entry points to scenes, texture, and a way to show institutional reality without narrating it.

---

## Clinic Documents

### Intake/Storage
- **Cryostorage Intake Form** — Initial registration of material
- **Third-Party Presence Log** — Record of non-patients present at procedures
- **Chain-of-Custody Log** — Who handled what, when
- **Storage Agreement** — Contract for ongoing storage
- **Storage Agreement Addendum** — Modifications (payment responsibility, contact info)

### Consent/Disposition
- **Consent Packet** — Full set of legal agreements
- **Disposition Election Form** — What happens to material if patient dies, becomes unreachable, etc.
- **Consent Update** — New version of consent language (jurisdiction changes, policy updates)
- **Consent Diff** — Comparison showing what changed between versions

### Ongoing Management
- **Annual Renewal Notice** — Reminder that storage continues, fees due
- **Payment Confirmation** — Receipt of fees
- **Contact Update Form** — Change of address, phone, email
- **Status Change Notice** — Account moved to Dormant, Delinquent, etc.

### Incidents/Transfers
- **Incident Report** — Mislabeled straw, freezer alarm, chain-of-custody ambiguity
- **Transfer Request** — Request to move material to another facility
- **Transfer Chain-of-Custody** — Documentation of physical move
- **Transfer Confirmation** — Material arrived, verified, logged

### Policy/Legal
- **Policy Memo** — Internal communication about procedure changes
- **Legal Notice** — Jurisdiction change, regulatory update
- **Compliance Audit** — Periodic verification of storage integrity

## Upload/Hosting Documents

- **Upload Consent** — Agreement for the procedure
- **Hosting Agreement** — Contract for mind-state infrastructure
- **Hosting Migration Notice** — Provider changing, account moving
- **Payment Due Notice** — Hosting fees
- **Terms of Service Update** — Changes to what hosting includes

---

## Communication Artifacts

- **Voicemail Transcript** — Captured audio, transcribed
- **Email** — Correspondence (formal or personal)
- **Chat Log** — Instant message records
- **Video Call Summary** — System-generated record of call metadata
- **Complaint Transcript** — Customer service interaction

---

## Personal Documents (Wren's)

- **Journal Entries** — Private writing, honest
- **Notes** — Fragments, reflections
- **Letters (unsent)** — Things written but never shared

---

## Usage Notes

- Artifacts should feel real—version numbers, dates, bureaucratic language
- They're entry points, not the whole scene
- What the system records badly is where meaning leaks through
- Redactions, missing fields, "see attached" with no attachment
- Policy version bumps that quietly change lives
- Artifacts can repeat across centuries with subtle drift (new headers, changed terminology)
